Let's talk about Ruby

I found a gem

Being an Object-Oriented Programming Developer for over 6 years made me curious about Ruby, it was new for me and I started digging what was that about. At the same time, a client needed a small web application to maintain the inventory of his watch store. It was an ideal match!

After I introduced Ruby on Rails (RoR) in my company and a new development methodology (Agile), I became their Technical lead for RoR. I did not expect to spend more than 13 years working with Ruby, RoR and a enormous bunch of the gems, it was a great surprise!

I revampireced old systems, I worked with cutting edge and ancient versions of Ruby and Ruby on Rails. Designing, creating, implementing and testing a large range of projects, which included CRMs, Financial and accountancy tools and Opensource Software for local farmers.
